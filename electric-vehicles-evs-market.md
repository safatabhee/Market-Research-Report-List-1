<p><p>Electric Vehicles (EVs) encompass various types, primarily Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs). BEVs operate solely on electric power, relying entirely on batteries for propulsion, which results in zero tailpipe emissions. In contrast, PHEVs combine an electric motor with a traditional internal combustion engine, allowing for both electric-only driving and gasoline use, thus providing flexibility and extended range. The market for these vehicles is increasingly expanding as consumers seek sustainable and cost-effective transportation solutions.</p></p>
