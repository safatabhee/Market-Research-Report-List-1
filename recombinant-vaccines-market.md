<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Recombinant Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subunit Recombinant Vaccines</li><li>Attenuated Recombinant Vaccines</li><li>Vector Recombinant Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recombinant vaccines market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Subunit Recombinant Vaccines** contain isolated proteins from pathogens, stimulating an immune response without using the whole pathogen. </p><p>2. **Attenuated Recombinant Vaccines** use genetically modified pathogens that are weakened, enabling them to provoke a robust immune response without causing disease. </p><p>3. **Vector Recombinant Vaccines** utilize harmless viruses or bacteria as vectors to deliver pathogen genes into the body, prompting an immune reaction. Each type plays a crucial role in disease prevention strategies.</p></p>
