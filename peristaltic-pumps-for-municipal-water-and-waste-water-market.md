<p><strong>The Peristaltic Pumps for Municipal Water and Waste Water Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Shoe type Peristaltic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are essential in municipal water and wastewater management, utilizing a unique mechanism to efficiently move fluids. Roller peristaltic pumps work by compressing a flexible hose with rotating rollers, creating a controlled flow suitable for various fluids. Shoe-type peristaltic pumps employ a shoe mechanism to exert pressure on the hose, providing gentle handling of viscous or shear-sensitive fluids. Both types offer reliable performance, corrosion resistance, and easy maintenance, making them ideal for demanding environmental conditions in municipal applications.</p></p>
