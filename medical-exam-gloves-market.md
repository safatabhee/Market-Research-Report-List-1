<p><p>The medical exam gloves market comprises three primary types: latex, vinyl, and nitrile gloves. Latex gloves offer excellent elasticity and sensitivity but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk tasks but provide less durability and protection compared to latex and nitrile. Nitrile gloves, made from synthetic rubber, are resistant to punctures and chemicals, making them ideal for various medical applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on protection, sensitivity, and risk levels.</p></p>
