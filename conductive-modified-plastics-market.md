<p><ul><li>Structural Conductive Plastic</li><li>Composite Conductive plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive modified plastics market comprises mainly two types: structural conductive plastics and composite conductive plastics. Structural conductive plastics are designed to provide both strength and electrical conductivity, often used in applications like automotive parts and electronic housings. Composite conductive plastics, on the other hand, integrate conductive fillers with a plastic matrix to enhance electrical properties while maintaining lightweight characteristics. These materials enable various industries, such as electronics and packaging, to achieve functionality without compromising performance.</p></p>
