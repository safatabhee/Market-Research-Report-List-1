<p><p>The Acute Surgical Mechanical Circulatory Support System market includes various types of devices designed to assist heart function during critical care. Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) devices provide oxygenation and circulation support by oxygenating blood outside the body, essential in cases of severe cardiac or respiratory failure. Extracorporeal Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) support heart function by mechanically pumping blood, often used in heart surgery or transplantation scenarios. Other devices may include intra-aortic balloon pumps and total artificial hearts, enhancing circulatory stability in emergencies.</p></p>
