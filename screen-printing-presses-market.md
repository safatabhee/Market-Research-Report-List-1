<p><ul><li>Flat-Bed Screen Printing Presses</li><li>Cylinder Screen Printing Presses</li><li>Rotary Screen Printing Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screen printing presses market is categorized into three main types: Flat-Bed, Cylinder, and Rotary. Flat-Bed screen printing presses use a stationary bed to hold the substrate, making them suitable for detailed designs on various materials. Cylinder screen printing presses utilize cylindrical screens for continuous printing, ideal for high-volume production and consistent patterns on items like bottles. Rotary screen printing presses offer high-speed, efficient printing on textiles and flexible materials through a rotating cylinder system, catering to large-scale manufacturing needs.</p></p>
