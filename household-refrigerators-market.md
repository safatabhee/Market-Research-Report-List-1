<p><p>The household refrigerators market includes various types catering to diverse needs. Compressor refrigerators, the most common, use a mechanical compressor for cooling. Absorption refrigerators utilize heat sources for operation, making them suitable for off-grid applications. Solar refrigerators harness solar energy for eco-friendly cooling solutions. Acoustic refrigerators leverage sound waves for temperature regulation, while magnetic refrigerators use magnetocaloric effects for efficient cooling. Thermal mass refrigerators rely on materials that store and release cold, promoting energy savings and sustainability in refrigeration technology.</p></p>
