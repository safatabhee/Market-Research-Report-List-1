<p><p>Non-spring return damper actuators are categorized based on their control types: </p><p>1. **Two-Position Control**: These actuators operate in two distinct states—fully open or fully closed—providing simple on/off control for HVAC systems.</p><p>2. **Three-Position Control**: This type allows for an intermediate position, enabling more precise control over airflow by adjusting the damper to a specified degree between open and closed.</p><p>3. **Others**: This category encompasses specialized actuators, including modulating controls, which provide continuous movement for varying airflow control in complex systems.</p></p>
