<p><p>The commercial vehicle battery market features primarily two types: Flooded Lead-acid Batteries and Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) Batteries. Flooded Lead-acid batteries are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and reliable performance, though they require maintenance and upright positioning. In contrast, AGM batteries offer superior vibration resistance, maintenance-free operation, and better deep-cycle capacity, making them ideal for demanding applications. Both types cater to different needs in commercial vehicles, balancing performance, reliability, and operational costs effectively.</p></p>
