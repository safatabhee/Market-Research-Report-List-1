<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMOS Image Transducers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI Technology</li><li>BSI Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS Image Transducers market is primarily segmented into Full-Stack Illuminated (FSI) and Backside Illuminated (BSI) technologies. FSI technology captures images by exposing the sensor's front side directly to light, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, BSI technology enhances sensitivity and performance by placing the photodetectors behind the circuitry, allowing more light to reach them. This results in improved low-light performance and higher image quality, making BSI increasingly popular in smartphones and cameras, thereby driving market growth.</p></p>
