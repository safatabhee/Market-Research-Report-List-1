<p><p>The milling tools market comprises various types designed for specific machining tasks. Recessing milling tools create indents or recesses in workpieces. Roughing milling tools remove material quickly, preparing surfaces for finishing. Contouring milling tools shape complex designs and curves. Face mills are used for flat surfaces, while semi-finishing cutters bridge the gap between roughing and finishing, providing a smoother finish. Threading milling tools are specialized for creating threads, ensuring precision in fastening applications. Each type is tailored for efficiency and accuracy in milling processes.</p></p>
