<p><strong>The Vehicle Horn Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electronic Horn</li><li>Electromagnetic Horns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle horn market comprises three main types: air horns, electronic horns, and electromagnetic horns. Air horns use compressed air to produce a loud sound, often favored in commercial vehicles for their powerful output. Electronic horns generate sound through a speaker system and are common in modern vehicles for their versatility and varied tone options. Electromagnetic horns, utilizing a mechanical diaphragm and electromagnet, offer reliability and are widely used in standard automobiles. Each type serves distinct safety and communication needs in vehicles.</p></p>
