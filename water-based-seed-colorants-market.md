<p><ul><li>Dyes</li><li>Pigments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-based seed colorants market comprises two primary types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants that bond with plant materials, providing a vibrant hue and facilitating uniform application on seeds. They are often used for aesthetic purposes or to signal specific crop traits. Pigments, on the other hand, are insoluble colorants that offer more durability and lightfastness, enhancing shelf life and resistance to environmental factors. Both types play a crucial role in seed identification and branding in agriculture.</p></p>
