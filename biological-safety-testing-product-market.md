<p><p>The biological safety testing product market encompasses various types of testing essential for ensuring the safety and quality of biopharmaceuticals and medical devices. Sterility testing products verify the absence of viable microbes, while endotoxin testing products detect harmful bacterial toxins. Cell line qualification assesses and characterizes cell lines used in research, and incidental reagent detection tests identify potential contaminants. Bioburden testing measures microbial load, and residual host contamination detection products identify traces of non-human materials, all crucial for regulatory compliance and product safety.</p></p>
