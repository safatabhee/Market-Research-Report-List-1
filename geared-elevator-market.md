<p><strong>The Geared Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geared elevator market features two primary types of operation: isokinetic and frequency conversion. Isokinetic operation maintains a constant velocity throughout the lift, ensuring smooth and efficient travel. This approach often enhances energy efficiency and reduces wear on components. In contrast, frequency conversion utilizes variable frequency drives to adjust motor speed, allowing for greater control over acceleration and deceleration. This technology improves energy savings and operational adaptability, catering to diverse building requirements and enhancing user comfort during elevation.</p></p>
