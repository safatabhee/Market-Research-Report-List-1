<p><strong>The Antihypertensive Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diuretic Antihypertensive</li><li>Sympathetic Suppressant</li><li>Renin-angiotensin System Inhibitor</li><li>Calcium Antagonist Medicine</li><li>vasodilator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antihypertensive drugs market includes various medication types that target high blood pressure. Diuretic antihypertensives help eliminate excess fluid and sodium, reducing blood volume. Sympathetic suppressants lower heart rate and cardiac output. Renin-angiotensin system inhibitors block hormone activity that narrows blood vessels, promoting dilation. Calcium antagonists prevent calcium from entering cells, easing vascular tension. Vasodilators directly relax blood vessels, improving blood flow. Each class plays a vital role in managing hypertension, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
