<p><p>The compression tape market is categorized into dynamic and static segments based on usage and application. Dynamic compression tapes are designed for activities involving movement, providing support during sports or physical therapy. They adapt to muscle and joint motion, enhancing performance and recovery. In contrast, static compression tapes are used for immobilization and injury management, maintaining pressure on the affected area without movement. Both types cater to different needs in rehabilitation, sports medicine, and general healthcare, reflecting their distinct functionalities.</p></p>
