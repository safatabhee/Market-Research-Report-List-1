<p><p>The current limit switches market is divided into two main types: fixed and adjustable current limit switches. Fixed current limit switches have a predetermined threshold for current, ensuring consistent protection in electrical systems. They are typically used in applications where the current demand is stable. In contrast, adjustable current limit switches allow users to modify the current limits as needed, providing flexibility for varying operational requirements. This adaptability makes them suitable for environments where current load fluctuates frequently.</p></p>
