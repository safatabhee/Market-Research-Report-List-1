<p><p>The drug labels market encompasses various types, primarily including pressure-sensitive labels and glue-applied labels. Glue-applied labels require a separate adhesive for adhesion, offering versatility in various environments but often require more complex application processes. Other labels may include heat shrink, in-mold, and fillable options, catering to specific needs in pharmaceutical branding, compliance, and product information dissemination, ensuring proper identification and safety.</p></p>
