<p><p>The refrigeration dryer market can be categorized into two main types: continuous and intermittent. Continuous refrigeration dryers operate non-stop, ensuring a steady removal of moisture from compressed air systems, making them ideal for applications requiring constant operation. In contrast, intermittent refrigeration dryers function on an on-demand basis, activating only when needed, which is suitable for scenarios with variable air requirements. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining air quality and preventing moisture damage across various industries.</p></p>
