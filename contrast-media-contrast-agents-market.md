<p><p>The contrast media and agents market comprises three main types: iodinated, gadolinium-based, and barium-based. Iodinated contrast agents are primarily used in X-ray and CT imaging, enhancing vascular structures. Gadolinium-based agents are commonly utilized in MRI scans to improve the visibility of soft tissues. Barium-based agents are specifically used for gastrointestinal studies, highlighting the digestive tract during X-ray exams. Each agent type serves distinct imaging modalities, optimizing diagnostic accuracy and patient safety in medical imaging applications.</p></p>
