<p><p>The Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) sensors market primarily includes two key types: Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS) and Inertial Navigation System (INS). AHRS provides orientation information by measuring angular velocity and magnetic field, commonly used in aviation and marine applications. INS, on the other hand, combines accelerometers and gyroscopes to calculate position, velocity, and orientation over time, essential for navigation in various vehicles, including aircraft and spacecraft, enabling precise movement tracking without external references.</p></p>
